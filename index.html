ViewerImage is a TagSpaces extension used for opening of the following image formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, SVG, BMP, WEBP, ICO. It supports zooming and rotating of the opened image. For JPG files it features an integrated EXIF parser used for the auto rotation of the pictures.
